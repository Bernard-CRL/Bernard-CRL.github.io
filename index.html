Goblin Machine

Goblin machine orgin point is from clash royale.

The Goblin Machine is a 5-elixir Legendary troop that can target both air and ground enemies, but in two very different ways. It switches between a melee mode for close-range encounters and a rocket launcher mode for ranged splash damage. This gives it a dual-threat capability not seen in most cards.

  • Melee Mode: Deals decent single-target damage to ground troops when enemies are within 2.5 tiles.
  • Rocket Mode: Launches explosive projectiles that deal area damage to both ground and air units from 2.5 to 5 tiles away.

This dynamic gives the card a unique identity. It can clear backline threats with rockets, then switch to slashing nearby enemies in the blink of an eye.
